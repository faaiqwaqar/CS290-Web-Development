Code Blog Entry One

Date: 4/8/2019 Assignment: 1

What challenges or troubles did you have completing this assignment? How did you solve them?

For me, the most challenging part of this assignment was understanding the requirements and formatting for my program. After having taken the CS 160 series, and programming in C, I have had a pretty good idea of what was expected of me in a programming assignment. When working with put html, I didnt know what was appropriate spacing, or commenting or what was even expected in titles. I asked my peers and the instructor to overcome this, and hope I will understand it better in the future.

What did you learn from this assignment? What did you already know?

I learned a basis of HTML text styles, and how to effectively use them to produce a basic looking page that can be set unto a server, which is more than what I knew to do before this initial assignment and handful of lectures.

What resources were most helpful in completing this assignment? How did you use these resources?

The most helpful resource for myself in this assignment was looking at piazza questions, and the guidance in the first set of lecture notes posted by the instructor. Piazza helped me understand issues I might have had and others were having and helped me not make those mistakes. The note packet helped me learn the basics of HTML that I used in this program.
